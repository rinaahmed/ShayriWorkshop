About S/L Rules
  • Every syllable is Short (S, 1 matra) or Long (L, 2 matras)
  • Syllable ending in consonant = Long (closed syllable)
  • Long vowels آ، او، ای = Long
  • Choti ye (ے) at end of syllable = Short
  • Bari ye (ی) = Long
  • Noon ghunna (ں) at end = Long
  • uthaana / uthaake / uthaaye = S-L-S (always)
